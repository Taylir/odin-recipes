Orange Chicken Parm

Description

Always having a soft spot for the hit youtube series good mythical morning and a big foodie when they added Good Mythical Kitchen to the bunch it was a home run. Not only that but the fact that the also combined some of my favorite foods into one made me have to make it. A mix betwen a chicken parm and orange chicken you would get from Panda express. This tomatoe, sugar, cheesy mixture is one to try over and over again with how well the flavors work together.

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • ¼ tsp white pepper
  • 1 Egg white
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Rice wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 pinch red chili flake
  • 1 Tbsp grated ginger
  • 12 ounces tomato juice
  • 2 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp cracked fennel seed
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • Deep fryer at 350 degrees
  • 10 cherry tomatoes cut in half
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Low-moisture mozzarella cut into small-ish cubes.
  1. Cut chicken thighs into chunks.
  2. Whisk together egg white, soy, sauce, pepper vinegar, and sugar.
  3. Add in chicken pieces and let marinate.
  4. Heat oil in a sautee pan and add ginger and garlic. Stir fry for a few minutes until fragrant. Add the rest of the ingredients except for (marinated chicken, 1 cup cornstarch, Fresh basil, Olive oil, Low-moisture mozzarella cut into small-ish cubes ) and cook for 6 minutes until sticky and reduced.
  5. Add cornstarch to chicken and stir until well-coated. Deep fry for 4 minutes, until golden brown, then remove and drain.
  6. Add chicken to the sticky tomato sauce and put it in a heat proof dish. Arrange halved cherry tomatoes around the saucy chicken and then add mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake at 500 degrees for 8 minutes, until cheese is melty and golden. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
